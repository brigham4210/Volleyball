About Volleyball Formation

Volleyball Formation Manager v1.0

A native app for creating and managing volleyball team formations.

Features:

  • 7 player positions with customizable labels
  • Drag and drop formation editing
  • Save and load formations
  • Quick formation presets

Default positions: O1, O2, M1, M2, S, OP, L
